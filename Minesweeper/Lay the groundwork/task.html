Description

Minesweeper is a nerve-racking game where you're presented with a field full of hidden mines. The goal is to find all of them and stay safe. It's not a game of wild guessing, so there are hints on the field: some cells show the number of mines around them. It's all about strategy!

Your first step is easy: you need to output some state of the field. The field contains mines marked as 'X' and safe cells marked as '.', but you can choose your own marking symbols. At this stage, all the mines are in plain sight, so you don't have to hide them from the user yet.
